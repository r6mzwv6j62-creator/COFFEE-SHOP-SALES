# COFFEE-SHOP-SALES


This project focuses on performing an end-to-end sales data analysis of a Coffee Shop using Microsoft Excel.
The objective was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights by building an interactive and visually appealing dashboard.
Through data cleaning, pivot tables, KPIs, and dynamic charts, this dashboard helps understand:
Customer purchasing behavior
Revenue trends
Peak business hours
Product performance
Location-wise footfall
Size-based order distribution


📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Average Bill Value: 4.69
Average Orders per Customer: 1.44
Total Footfall: 149,116
Total Revenue: $698,812.33
These KPIs provide a quick summary of overall business performance.
📈 Detailed Analysis Performed



🔹 1. Hourly Sales Trend Analysis
Analyzed quantity ordered based on hours of the day to identify peak sales timings.
✔ Peak demand observed during morning hours (8 AM – 10 AM)
✔ Useful for optimizing staffing and operational efficiency



🔹 2. Product Category Revenue Contribution
Compared revenue contribution across categories such as:
Coffee
Tea
Bakery
Drinking Chocolate
Coffee Beans
Branded Products
✔ Coffee category contributes the highest share of total revenue
✔ Helps in prioritizing high-performing categories



🔹 3. Size-wise Order Distribution
Analyzed order distribution across:
Small
Regular
Large
Not Defined
✔ Regular size dominates order volume
✔ Supports pricing and inventory decisions



🔹 4. Location-wise Footfall Comparison
Compared store performance across:
Astoria
Hell’s Kitchen
Lower Manhattan
✔ Identified highest footfall generating location
✔ Supports expansion and marketing strategy decisions



🔹 5. Top 5 Best-Selling Products
Identified top revenue-generating products such as:
Barista Espresso
Brewed Chai Tea
Hot Chocolate
Gourmet Brewed Coffee
Brewed Black Tea
✔ Helps focus on high-demand products



🔹 6. Day-wise Sales Performance
Analyzed sales performance across different days to determine highest revenue-generating day.
✔ Supports promotional planning and staffing allocation
